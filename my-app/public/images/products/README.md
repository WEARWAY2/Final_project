## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, JPEG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended Size**: 800x1000px (4:5 aspect ratio)
- **Maximum Size**: 2MB per image
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit product photos
- **Background**: White or neutral background preferred

## Image Naming Convention

Format: `[category]-[product-name]-[number].jpg`

Examples:

- `tshirt-gradient-1.jpg` - First image of Gradient T-shirt
- `polo-tipping-2.jpg` - Second image of Polo with Tipping Details
- `dress-floral-3.jpg` - Third image of Floral Summer Dress

## Placeholder Images

Currently using placeholder paths. To add real images:

1. Place your product images in the appropriate category folder (men/women/kids)
2. Follow the naming convention above
3. Images will automatically load from `/images/products/[category]/[filename]`

## Tips for Product Photography

1. **Lighting**: Use natural or bright, even lighting
2. **Angles**: Include front view, back view, and detail shots
3. **Model/Mannequin**: Show products worn or styled
4. **Details**: Close-up shots of fabric, buttons, patterns
5. **Consistency**: Use similar backgrounds and lighting for all products
